BeFunky

The Good

Photo editor, collage maker, designer. You can upload photos from different platforms. It is a fairly clear software and it is easy to learn where everything can be found.

The Bad

The basic supplies are free -let’s say the necessary options- until you want something more special and you have to pay for it, without having a subscription. If you want higher resolution images output and just extra options, like filters and edit filters, you have to pay 3 dollars a month.

The Bottom Line

If you want to edit your photos quickly and without any effort, such as learning Photoshop, for example, this is the perfect ‘start’ program. It is simple and usable for everyone. With this web software you can quickly and easily perform fun tests on your photo or even design your own collage and menu cards.
