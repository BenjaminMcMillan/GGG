Vectr

The Good

The software is internet based so you can access your files wherever you are. It's easy to use and functions very well. It's a good alternative for paid vector software.

The Bad

It must have an internet connection or the software doesn't work. Sometimes the software is slow.

The Bottom Line

The internet plays a big role in this tool. It all depends on that. You can't access your files if you don't have internet.
